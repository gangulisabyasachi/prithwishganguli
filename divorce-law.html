Divorce Law

Overview

Expert guidance in all aspects of matrimonial law, including mutual consent divorces, contested divorces, and family matters. With 25+ years of experience, we've successfully handled hundreds of divorce cases.

Grounds for Divorce

  • Adultery
  • Cruelty
  • Desertion
  • Conversion to another religion
  • Unsoundness of mind
  • Leprosy or venereal disease
  • Mutual consent

Divorce Procedures

Contested Divorce: Requires proof of grounds and can take 2-5 years depending on complexity.

Mutual Consent Divorce: Faster process (6 months minimum) when both parties agree.
